# LinkedIn Video Series Storyboard: "The AI-Powered Professional Stack"

## Series Overview
**Theme:** How professionals can leverage AI infrastructure for career transformation
**Total Episodes:** 10 video segments across 5 topic areas
**Format:** LinkedIn-optimized vertical video (9:16) + supporting carousel images (4:5)
**Generation Pipeline:** Wan 2.1/2.6 for video, FLUX 1.0 for images

---

## Visual Design System

### Color Palette
- **Primary Background:** Deep charcoal (#1a1a1a)
- **Accent Warm:** Amber gold (#d4a373)
- **Accent Cool:** Electric teal (#4ecdc4)
- **Text Primary:** Off-white (#f5f5f5)
- **Text Secondary:** Silver gray (#a0a0a0)

### Typography Direction
- Headlines: Bold condensed sans-serif (editorial authority)
- Body: Clean geometric sans
- Technical callouts: Monospace for specs

### Visual Motifs
- Subtle grid overlays suggesting data/connectivity
- Ambient light effects (warm amber glow on hardware)
- Floating particle systems for AI/processing scenes
- Clean product photography aesthetic

## POST-PRODUCTION GUIDELINES

### Text Styling
- Headlines: Bold condensed, 72-96pt, off-white
- Subheads: Regular weight, 48-64pt, amber accent
- Specs: Monospace, 36-48pt, teal accent
- All text: 2px dark drop shadow for readability

### Animation Principles
- Text entrance: Fade up + slight scale (0.3s ease-out)
- Text exit: Fade down (0.2s)
- Spec numbers: Typewriter reveal effect
- Transitions: Cross-dissolve (0.5s)

### Audio Direction
- Background: Subtle electronic ambient (tech documentary style)
- Transitions: Soft digital whoosh
- Text reveals: Subtle UI click
- No voiceover (let visuals speak)

### Export Specifications
- LinkedIn Video: 9:16, 1080×1920, H.264, 30fps
- LinkedIn Carousel Images: 4:5, 1080×1350, PNG
- Color Space: sRGB
- Duration target: 15-30 seconds per video
